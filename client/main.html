How to play:

2 teams take turns guessing the word or phrase on the screen. On each turn, one player will give clues about the word/phrase to her teammates. A 60 second timer begins when the "Start" button is pressed. The team not guessing when the timer runs out gets a point.

Tap "Next" when your team has guessed the current word. Tap "Skip" to get a new word for your team to guess.
